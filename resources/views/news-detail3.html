Application note in daily life:

In daily life, porcelain or glass tableware should be used as much as possible, followed by disposable transparent plastic products, and try not to use the paper cup series.

Harm of inferior paper cups:

We first break down the inferior paper cup. Its composition is: paper, printing ink, industrial paraffin, and coated paper. Let's explain it in turn.

I. Paper: Many manufacturers do not use food grade wood pulp paper for maximum benefit. The shoddy is everywhere. If the paper quality is poor (recycled waste paper), bleaching powder and fluorescent whitening agent are used, which is unrecognizable to ordinary consumers.

Second, printing ink: In the era when production costs are everything, the national standards for printing inks seem to be ineffective. Therefore, inks containing benzene and toluene are also flooding the market. After the printed paper cups are stacked, the ink is likely to contaminate the inner wall of the paper cup.

Third, industrial paraffin: If the paper cup is not waterproof, can it still contain water? Therefore, there is a paper cup that has been waxed. The wax contains a harmful substance called "polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons", and the melting point of the wax is 40 °, and the oil can dissolve the wax, as you know.

Fourth, coated paper: Generally speaking, everyone is so called, but it is actually a polyethylene plastic film. Many paper cups have replaced paraffin with coated paper. However, the outer wall of the "protective" cup without paraffin may be moldy after damp, and the inner wall of the cup will be contaminated with mold. Regarding this plastic film, if it is not a food grade material, harmful substances are decomposed at high temperatures.

In addition, inferior paper cups were found to contain bisphenol A. According to the determination of bisphenol A content in some daily necessities by experts from Nanjing Medical University, the results showed that the bisphenol A content in the disposable paper cup was 30 times that of the disposable plastic cup, and the bisphenol A content in the rainwater was More than five times the pure water in the water dispenser.
